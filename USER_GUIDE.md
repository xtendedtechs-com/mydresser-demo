## ü™û Virtual Try-On (MyMirror)

See how items look before buying with AI-powered virtual try-on.

### How to Use

1. **Upload Your Photo**
   - Take a clear, full-body photo
   - Good lighting, neutral background
   - Stand straight facing camera
   - One-time setup (photo saved securely)

2. **Select Items**
   - Browse your wardrobe items
   - Try marketplace items before buying
   - Mix and match multiple pieces
   - See complete outfit combinations

3. **Generate Try-On**
   - Tap "Try On" button
   - AI processes in 10-20 seconds
   - View realistic rendering
   - Compare multiple options side-by-side

### Tips for Best Results

- **Photo Quality:**
  - Use well-lit area
  - Plain background (white/light color)
  - Wear fitted clothing for accuracy
  - Full body visible

- **Item Selection:**
  - Try items in your size range
  - Similar style to photo clothing
  - Clear item photos work best

- **Limitations:**
  - Works best with tops and outerwear
  - Accessories may vary in accuracy
  - Lighting may differ from actual garment
